About

We don’t give our members what they want. We give them what they need.

There’s no shortage of gyms and trainers in Chicago offering the latest, trendy workout. But fitness shouldn’t be a trend—it should be a lifelong practice that continually evolves.

         We improve the quality of our members’ lives by offering lasting fitness support that encourages physical and mental well-being. Our approach is to take each of our members’ down the correct path, which isn’t always the shortest or easiest one.

With these beliefs in mind, we’ve created a private environment where we focus on movement patterns that build strength, endurance, flexibility, mobility, and stability. Each member is given the tools to make sustainable and impactful change in a setting that fosters trust and organic growth.

training

Our approach consists of three key areas, which together create scientifically sound, sustainable workouts focused on improving functional movement.

ASSESS

Assesment & regular fit-test

Membership at Fulton Fit House begins with an initial assessment. We get to know you: your prior workout history, injuries and any sourness This allows us to create a customized workout plan that is specific to your individual goals and abilities.

ENGAGE

Cardio, Strength-training & Diet

Either with a group or privately with our certified coaches, we implement functional fitness through traditional strength and endurance training coupled with analytics-driven testing.

PERFORM

Marathons & Obstacle courses

We strongly encourage all members to put their training to the test through marathons, competitive sports, obstacle courses— anything that seemed unattainable to you before. Once you conquer your fitness goals, who knows what else you can accomplish?
