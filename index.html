ABOUT



Philosophy

We don’t give our members what they want. We give them what they need.

There’s no shortage of gyms and trainers in Chicago offering the latest, trendy workout. But fitness shouldn’t be a trend—it should be lifelong practice that continually evolves. We want to improve the quality of our members’ lives by offering lasting fitness support that encourages physical and mental well-being. Our approach is to take each of our members down the correct path, which isn’t always the shortest or easiest one.

With these beliefs in mind, we’ve created a private environment where we focus on building strength, endurance, flexibility, mobility, and stability. Each member is given the tools to make sustainable and impactful change, in a setting that fosters trust and organic growth

    TRAINING





    Our Approach



    Our approach consists of three key areas, which together create scientifically sound, sustainable workouts focused on improving functional movement.



    ASSESS




    Assesment & fit-test


    Membership at Fulton Fit House begins with an initial assessment. We get to know you: your prior workout history, injuries and any sourness. This allows us to create a customized workout plan that is specific to your individual goals and abilities.

  2. 2

    ENGAGE



    Cardio, Strength-training & Diet

    Either with a group or privately with our certified coaches, we implement functional fitness through traditional strength and endurance training coupled with analytics-driven testing.

    PERFORM



    Races, Marathons & Obstacle courses

    We stronglly encourage all members to put our training to the test throungh marathons, competative sports, obstacle cources or any other things out side of your comfort zone. Once you accomplish what you want with fitness who knows what else you can accomplish.
